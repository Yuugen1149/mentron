The MENTRON Initiative ‚Äî Bridging Knowledge Gaps

The landscape of academic success is witnessing a paradigm shift. With the introduction of MENTRON, students are now empowered more than ever to bridge the gap between confusion and clarity. "It is not just about passing exams," says the initiative lead, "It is about truly understanding and growing together."

The initiative connects juniors with seniors who transform experience into guidance. Providing access to previous year questions, curated notes, and smart study resources, MENTRON ensures no student walks the academic path alone.

From peer mentoring and doubt-clearing sessions to exam preparation strategies, the platform supports communication skills, confidence building, and interview readiness.
